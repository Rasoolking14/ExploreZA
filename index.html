Our story

Adventure shouldn't be a privilege

The problem we solve

Most South African kids never get to explore their own country

For learners in poor communities and under-resourced schools, a day in the mountains, a trip to the coast, or a wildlife reserve is simply out of reach. There's no money for transport, gear, or guides — so generation after generation grows up cut off from the natural beauty on their doorstep.

ExploreZA exists to close that gap. We're a non-profit that funds and runs safe outdoor adventures for these learners at no cost to them or their schools — paid for by everyday donors and corporate partners.

  • Free for every learner and school we work with
  • Safe, guided trips with transport, meals and gear included
  • Funded by donations and corporate CSI partnerships
  • Full impact reporting back to every funder
